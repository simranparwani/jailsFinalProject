INTRODUCTION

Discrimination in law enforcement, especially in the United States, has been a prominent topic of conversation for decades. People argue that POC are more likely to be detained by the police, they are more likely to be arrested, and that their bails are set higher when compared to other white criminals. In any given year, approximately 11 million Americans are detained in county jails, and yet we know little about local jail practices.
